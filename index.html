About Us

At Coffee House in Dhaka,Bangladesh,we pride ourselves on being a go to destination for free coffee lovers and conversation seekers alike.We’re dedicated to sustainability. From ethically sourcing our beans to using eco-friendly cups and straws, we strive to reduce our environmental impact while supporting farmers who share our commitment to quality and ethical practices.
